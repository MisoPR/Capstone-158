ABOUT US

The sun is our primary source of renewable energy, due to its higher irradiance at the location of Puerto Rico it can be crucial
for the improvement of the electric grid in the island. Increases in electric bills,
lack of good administration throughout the years, continuous blackouts, and natural disasters
such as hurricanes have made the grid unreliable. In addition, excessive reliance on fossil fuels
for electricity generation has been a significant problem recently because of warlike
conflicts between major distributors incrementing its cost and the environmental pollution that it causes.
Therefore, PV systems have become a viable solution for many customers and for the industrial sector with power hungry companies.
